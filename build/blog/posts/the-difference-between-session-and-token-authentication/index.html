Difference Between Session and Token Authentication

1 min read 28 Jul 2021web-dev

User Authentication is the act of allowing users who meet a certain criteria to view or access certain parts of a website or webapp and filter out those who dont.
If you log in into your Facebook account, that is is authentication. Authentication serves the same primary purpose, to allow in allowed users and lock out those who aren't allowed. But the methods of doing this differs.
In this article I will cover Session and Token Authentication.
